Benefits of Modals


Simplicity
Everything stays within one tab, helping visitors stay connected with what they were doing before the modal appeared.

Visibility
Since modals appear within the userâ€™s active tab, you can be sure it will be seen. If instead the prompt were to appear in a new window, the user could miss it or instinctively close the new window after being conditioned by annoying ad popups.

Flexibility
Modals also help preserve page space by displaying featured media, like images or videos, in a lightbox.

Pop Up vs Modal


If the user chooses not to interact with a popup, they are free to continue interacting with the site while the popup remains on the screen until the user engages with it. In contrast, modals do not allow you to interact with other elements on the page, which necessitates an immediate action on the user's part.
